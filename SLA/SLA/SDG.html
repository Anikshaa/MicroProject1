SDG 3: Good Health and Well-Being

Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages

About SDG 3

Sustainable Development Goal 3 aims to ensure health and well-being for all, at every stage of life. The goal addresses all major health priorities, including reproductive, maternal, and child health; communicable, non-communicable, and environmental diseases; universal health coverage; and access for all to safe, effective, quality, and affordable medicines and vaccines.

Key Targets

Maternal Health

Reduce global maternal mortality ratio to less than 70 per 100,000 live births

Child Mortality

End preventable deaths of newborns and children under 5 years of age

Infectious Diseases

End epidemics of AIDS, tuberculosis, malaria, and neglected tropical diseases

Non-Communicable Diseases

Reduce premature mortality from non-communicable diseases by one-third

Mental Health

Promote mental health and well-being, including substance abuse treatment

Universal Health Coverage

Achieve universal health coverage and access to quality healthcare services
