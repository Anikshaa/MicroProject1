DO's in First Aid

  • Call for help immediately.
  • Ensure your safety first.
  • Stay calm and reassure the victim.
  • Wear protective gloves.
  • Check for responsiveness and breathing.
  • Control severe bleeding.
  • Use the recovery position.
  • Start CPR if not breathing normally.
  • Use an AED if available.
  • Cover wounds to prevent infection.
  • Keep the injured person warm.
  • Document important information.
  • Be extra gentle with children.

DON'Ts in First Aid

  • Don't panic.
  • Don't move a seriously injured person unless necessary.
  • Don't try to set a broken bone.
  • Don't remove embedded objects.
  • Don't give food or drink to an unconscious person.
  • Don't apply ice directly to frostbite.
  • Don't apply tourniquets if untrained.
  • Don't attempt CPR if untrained (unless guided).
  • Don't assume an unconscious person is asleep.
  • Don't share personal medications.
